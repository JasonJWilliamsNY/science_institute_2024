Quiz Review Sheet

Quiz: Lab Safety and Bacterial Transformation

This is your first quiz so here are some ground rules/instructions.

  1. No talking - During the quiz there is absolutely no talking. If you have a question, please raise your hand. If I can fairly answer your question (for example about a ambiguous statement or error) I will and will inform the other quiz takers.
  2. Spread out - you must have an empty chair to your right and left.
  3. You must bring your Chromebook - quiz will be online
  4. When the music stops you must submit your quiz - more info on this in class.

Review Materials and General Quiz Questions

This quiz is has some memorization. Be familiar and able to define the following terms. Your study time should be 20-30 minutes. We will have time in class to answer specific questions before the quiz.

  • See our safety notes, Measurement notes
  • pipetting lab demo
  • Slides Intro to DNA Slides Slides 7-17.

General quiz questions

You will be expected to comment on

  1. MSDS (What are they? What basic information they tells us?)
  2. What is PPE? (Give some examples of protective equipment)
  3. Pipette basics (units of volumes, setting, proper handling)
  4. What is the difference between accuracy and precision
  5. The basic structure of DNA, the relationship of DNA and chromosomes, and the relationship between DNA, RNA, and Proteins.
