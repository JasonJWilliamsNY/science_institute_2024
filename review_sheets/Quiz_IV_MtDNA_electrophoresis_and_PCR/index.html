Ground rules/instructions

  1. Virtual quiz - instructions provided in email

Review

The following will be important to review for this quiz, use these when considering the example topics/questions below:

  • mtDNA notes
  • mtDNA slides
  • mtDNA lab protocol (your lab notes)
  • 2D DNA sequencing animation/guide

Here are the important topics/questions you should know for the quiz (Note: We will not cover DNA sequencing on this quiz).

  • How DNA was isolated for mtDNA sequencing (e.g. saline rinse and chelex)
  • Distinct features of mtDNA (e.g. size, shape, inheritance)
  • What region of mtDNA was amplified and why
  • The general steps of Sanger DNA sequencing
  • What is the importance of ddNTPs in DNA sequencing
  • What are Phred scores and why are they important for interpreting DNA sequence?
  • What is a BLAST search?
