DNA Basics

In this course, we a grounded in molecular biology, an understanding of living organisms at the level of molecules. This is a fascinating topic (your instructor may be biased) as it lies at the intersection of physics, chemistry, and life. While you will go on to further your studies in other areas of science (hopefully returning here in the future), we will have many opportunities to explore science through this lense.
