Lecture

DNA Barcoding

DNA Barcodes allow us to use DNA to identify organisms and/or items that were once living. While the concept may sound straightforward, not every DNA sequence is a useful barcode. We will develop our final class project by getting you to identify plants using both barcodes and taxonomic methods - combining evidence from DNA as well as observation.
