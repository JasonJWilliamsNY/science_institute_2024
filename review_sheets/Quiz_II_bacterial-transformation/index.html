Quiz Review Sheet

Quiz: Bacterial transformation results

Ground rules/instructions

  1. No talking - During the quiz there is absolutely no talking. If you have a question, please raise your hand. If I can fairly answer your question (for example about a ambiguous statement or error) I will and will inform the other quiz takers.
  2. Spread out - you must have an empty chair to your right and left.
  3. You must bring your Chromebook - quiz will be online
  4. When the music stops you must submit your quiz - more info on this in class.

Review

Review the following

  • Notes on bacterial transformation
  • Bacterial transformation slides I
  • Bacterial transformation slides II
  • Restriction enzyme movie
  • Heat Shock animation (We will cover before the quiz, be familiar but quiz will not be super-detailed)

We will NOT cover: Slides on experimental controls; Uses of GFP (BT II slides) will be covered before the quiz.

Sample Question areas

  • Know what is a model organism and features do model organisms typically have.
  • Know some of the ways we can use sterile techniques we use when working with bacteria.
  • Know what a plasmid is and how to interpret a plasmid map.
  • Know how restriction enzymes and ligase are used to construct plasmids.
  • Know what is a bacterial colony.
  • Know the basic steps of heat shock.
  • Know what GFP is.
  • Be able to explain why we see the results we did on the set of LB/LB+AMP. plates when our +pGFP/-pGFP bacteria were applied to the petri plates.
